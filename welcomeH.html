You Are Welcome Here

Church Service

By observing the Sabbath day as holy, we gather as one body of Christ to praise God. This sacred time strengthens our faith as we hear and receive the Gospel, uniting us in devotion and spiritual growth. Our church offers two Sunday services, one from 8 a.m. to 10 a.m., and another from 10 a.m. to 12 p.m.

Prayer Meeting

When we unite in prayer, we encounter the presence of God. As stated in Matthew 18:20 , "where two or three gather in His name, He is there among them". Additionally, Ephesians 3:12 assures us that through Christ and our faith in Him, we can approach God's presence with boldness and confidence. The prayer meetings are scheduled for Wednesday for face-to-face gatherings and Thursday for virtual meetings, both starting at 6 p.m.

Lifegroup

LifeGroups in Kingdom Life serve the purpose of discovering freedom in Christ, fostering connections among participants, and experiencing collective growth. These groups are segmented based on age, catering to youth, young adults, and adults, facilitating relatability and meaningful interactions as individuals share their experiences.

Children Ministries

Through children ministries we let young children develop a relationship with God and encounter His love, echoing the words of Jesus: "Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these."

Night Life

An evening designed for youth to strengthen their connection with God and form new friendships, filled with uplifting Worship, insightful Word, and delightful fellowship. This event occurs every Friday at 6 in the evening.

BABAD

"BABAD," which stands for "Soaking," is a monthly event held every last Sunday of the month. It provides an opportunity to immerse oneself in the presence of God, seeking His comfort and experiencing His divine presence.
